shots from
around the world

and how to make them

Each shot is either inspired by a popular drink in a country or is actually from the country.

Even though we can't travel, we can taste different flavours of the world.


Please drink responsibly.

Did you know?

Shots are different sizes in different places in the world. Every recipe in this page will follow these size guidelines:

Shot

40ml or 1.4oz

Big Shot

71ml or 2.5oz

Ready to give it a shot?

Melktertjies

Makes about 20 shots

One 375 ml bottle Vodka
One 395 gram (300ml) tin of condensed milk
Half of a 375 ml tin evaporated milk
Ground cinnamon

1. Mix the vodka, condensed milk, and evaporated milk in a big bowl, or, if you want to shake it up a bit, pour everything into a one litre bottle and shake
2. Chill the mix before pouring out into your shot glass of choice
3. Sprinkle each melktertjie with ground cinnamon

Serve chilled

Apple Pie Bourbon Shot

Makes about 6 shots

1 1/2 cups of bourbon
2 apples chopped
3 tbsps sugar
1 whole nutmeg, slightly shaved
4 cinnamon sticks, plus more for garnish
1" piece fresh ginger

1. Fill a jar or container with bourbon, apples, sugar, nutmeg, cinnamon, and ginger
2. Let mixture sit for 2 to 4 days, depending on how strong you want it
3. Pour into shot glasses and garnish with cinnamon sticks

Pisco Sour

Makes 3 shots

140ml Pisco (Amber Brandy)
3 tbsps lime juice
1 egg white
30ml simple syrup (boil 1 part sugar with 1 part water)
Bitters

1. Shake up Pisco, lime juice, egg white, simple syrup, then add ice and shake again
2. Strain into shot glasses
3. Garnish with two drops of bitters and a lime slice

Serve chilled

Caesar Shooter

Makes 12 shots

Celery salt and lime juice (for the rim)
One and a half cups clamato juice
One quarter cup vodka
Half a teaspoon worcestershire sauce
Tabasco (two dashes or more)
Celery, shrimp, olives, or other garnishes

1. Rim shot glasses with lime juice and celery salt (use two plates and dip)
2. Either stir or shake the clamato juice, vodka, worcestershire sauce, and tabasco then pour into shot glasses
3. Garnish to your liking

Cucumber Sake Shot

Makes 4 shots

Half a cucumber, peeled
50ml Sake
100ml rice vodka or regular vodka (optional)
One tbsp sugar
Juice of half a lime

1. Muddle or blend peeled cucumber, lime juice, and sugar then strain out the pulp
2. Mix or shake half a cup of cucumber lime juice with Sake and vodka
3. Strain into shot glasses and garnish with cucumber

Serve chilled

Cherry Fernet con Coca

Makes 4 big shots

4 Tbsp Grenadine
160ml Fernet-Branca
120ml Coca-Cola

1. Layer a spoon of grenadine into each shot
2. Stir together Coca-Cola and Fernet with ice then pour over grenadine

Serve chilled

Baby Guiness

Makes 1 big shot

60ml Coffee liquor
11ml Irish cream liquor

1. Pour coffee liquor into shot glass
2. Use the back of a spoon to layer irish cream liquor over coffee liquor

Bandera

Makes 1 serving of 3 shots

One shot of lime juice
One shot of tequila
One shot of sangrita (Half orange juice, half spicy tomato juice, and a bit of lime juice)

1. Set out three shot glasses, fill the first with lime juice and the second with tequila
2. Make sangrita by mixing together orange juice, tomato juice, and lime, then pour into third shot glass

Intended to be drank by sipping between tequila, lime, then sangrita)

Pimm's Shooter

Makes about 4 big shots

120ml Pimm's
120ml Plymouth Gin
80ml simple syrup
40ml lemon juice
Cucumber and strawberry slices

1. Muddle 3 cucumber slices and 2 strawberries
2. Mix in ice, Pimm's, gin, simple syrup and lemon juice, then shake or stir
3. Place strawberry and cucumber slices on shot glasses
4. Strain drink into each shot glass

Serve chilled

Strawberry Sangria Shot

Makes about 12 shots

1 1/2 cups red wine
1/4 cup brandy
1/4 cup flavoured vodka (preferably berry)
8 strawberries (for soaking/garnish)
1 orange (for soaking)

1. Pour wine, brandy, and vodka with ice in a container with sliced orange and sliced strawberries
2. Let the mixture soak for at least 2 hours, up to overnight
3. Pour into shot glasses and garnish with strawberry

Serve chilled

Blue Banana

Makes 1 big shot

23ml blue curacoa
23ml banana liqueur
23ml Ouzo

1. Shake or stir together equal parts ouzo, blue curacoa, and banana liquor
2. Pour into a shot glass

Serve chilled

Zombie Shot

Makes two big shots

15ml Jamaican rum
30ml Gold rum
30ml Cherry brandy
30ml White rum
Splash of Bacardi 151
Grenadine
30ml Pineapple juice and mango juice

1. Mix or shake gold rum, white rum, brandy, mango and pineapple juice
2. Pour mixture evenly into two shot glasses
3. Layer Jamaican rum over mixture using back of spoon
4. Top with a splash of Bacardi 151 and pour grenadine in from the edge
5. Light on fire (blow out before drinking)
